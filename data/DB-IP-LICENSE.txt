Refreshing the database:
  The DB-IP free database is updated monthly. To update the bundled copy:

    YYYYMM=2026-07   # update to the target month
    curl -L -o /tmp/dbip.mmdb.gz \
      "https://download.db-ip.com/free/dbip-country-lite-${YYYYMM}.mmdb.gz"
    gunzip -c /tmp/dbip.mmdb.gz > data/dbip-country-lite.mmdb
    # update the version line in this file, then commit

For a richer City-level database (adds city names):
  Download the DB-IP City Lite .mmdb from https://db-ip.com/db/download/ip-to-city-lite
  and set GEOIP_DB_PATH=/path/to/dbip-city-lite.mmdb before launching the app.
  MaxMind GeoLite2-City.mmdb (same format) also works if placed at
  /usr/share/GeoIP/GeoLite2-City.mmdb or via GEOIP_DB_PATH.
